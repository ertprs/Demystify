Demystify FEMA

“Learn the gamut of Foreign Exchange Law in India”

Demystify FEMA refers to decoding the most complex law in India i.e. Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA). A one stop destination for real-time updates on Foreign Exchange Law in India and single platform tool for all the professionals comprising of Company Secretaries, Chartered Accountants and Lawyers which equips them with expert knowledge on the subject; helping them understand the complexities of Foreign Exchange law thereby finding out the right solution for their clients.

Key Website Features
  • FEMA Manualcomprising of FEMA Regulations, FEMA Notifications, AP DIR Circulars, Foreign Direct Investment Policy / Circular, DIPP Press Notes, Master Directions and Master Circulars
  • Compounding Penalty Calculatorfor calculating penalties for any contraventions/violations committed under Foreign Exchange Law
  • Compare Featuresi.e. comparison of FEMA Regulations with Master Directions or Foreign Direct Investment Policy / Circular
  • Post A Query Featurei.e. Subscribed Users will be able to post their basic queries on matters concerning Foreign Exchange Law in India.
